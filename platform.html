The Republic Platform

Our comprehensive blueprint for a stronger Republic. We are committed to economic justice, uncompromising national security, robust democratic institutions, and seamlessly connecting every community through modern infrastructure and localized innovation.

Economic Justice & Local Commerce

We will bring back taxation for luxury goods and implement a robust wealth tax to ensure the ultra-rich pay their fair share. Simultaneously, we support our foundational smaller businesses by lowering their taxes for each employee they employ, directly incentivizing local job creation and community growth.

National Security & Intelligence Reform

The defense of the Republic requires a unified approach. We will repeal the Nuremberg Act, which unjustly punishes the Caprican Office of Intelligence (COIN). To create a streamlined and impenetrable intelligence apparatus, we will merge domestic security (CIVIC) back into COIN.

Judicial Expansion & Balance

Our courts must reflect the true scope and needs of our growing Republic. We pledge to nominate Mary Reichel Proctor to the Supreme Court and actively expand the court's seats to ensure a balanced, robust, and profoundly fair judicial system that works for all Capricans.

Higher Education & Innovation

Education and innovation are the twin engines of the Republic. We are committing to massively increased funding for higher education. Furthermore, we will establish a dedicated Research & Development (R&D) Center in every state. These centers will be staffed first by the local community and will prioritize solving the unique industrial and ecological needs of their respective regions.

Rural Infrastructure & Logistics

Our economy relies on seamless connectivity. Under our new CapriConnect infrastructure plan, we will aggressively expand the infrastructure of rural nodes for commercial and logistical use. The core aim of CapriConnect is to link every single state across the Republic with a modernized high-speed rail network, ensuring no region is left behind.
